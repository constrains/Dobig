About Us

DoBig Studios

Created in 2020, Do Big Studios creates, develops, manages, and markets games on the Roblox platform. Our games have been played over 4 billion times, with over 80 million monthly active users. Games that we have worked on include Blade Ball, Obby But You're on a Bike, Color or Die, Sword Fighters Simulator, Race Clicker, Clicker Simulator, and many more. We have sold games, partnered, provide consulting and marketing for industry leading studios and implemented advertising campaigns for renowned companies such as Nickelodeon, Lunchables and the American Heart Association.
